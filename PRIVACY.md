# Privacy Policy for F1 Blocker Chrome Extension

**Last Updated:** November 22, 2024

## Overview

F1 Blocker ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension that blocks the F1 key from opening the Chrome help tab. This privacy policy explains what information the Extension collects, how it's used, and your rights regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Extension Settings
- **Blocking Preference**: Whether F1 blocking is enabled or disabled
- **Storage Location**: Chrome's sync storage (if sync is enabled) or local storage
- **Purpose**: To remember your blocking preference between browser sessions

### 2. What We Do NOT Collect
- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No website content
- No keystrokes (except F1 detection for blocking)
- No user identifiers
- No location data
- No analytics or tracking data

## How We Use Information

### Extension Settings
Your blocking preference is used solely to:
- Remember whether you want F1 blocking enabled
- Apply your preference when the browser starts
- No other processing or analysis is performed

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Method
- Data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- If Chrome sync is enabled, settings may sync across your Chrome instances
- No external servers or databases are used

### Data Retention
- Settings are stored until you uninstall the extension
- You can clear data by uninstalling the extension

## Permissions Explanation

### Required Permissions
- **`storage`**: To save your blocking preference
- **`webNavigation`**: To detect when Chrome help pages are being opened

### What Permissions Do NOT Allow
- Cannot read your browsing history
- Cannot access website content
- Cannot track your activities
- Cannot collect personal information

## Third-Party Sharing

**We do not share any data with third parties.**
- No data is sent to external servers
- No analytics services are used
- No advertising networks are integrated

## User Rights

You have the right to:
- View what data is stored (via Chrome's extension storage)
- Delete all data by uninstalling the extension
- Disable the extension at any time
- Choose whether to enable F1 blocking

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will:
- Update the "Last Updated" date
- Notify users through the extension description
- Provide clear information about what changed

## Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the extension's support channels.

## Compliance

This extension complies with:
- Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements
